Purpose

The purpose of the site is to connect people who needs someone to take care of their Pets while they are away from home. The website will contain pet tutors and their schedule. The cutomers will be able to take their pets to the tutor's place or have them coming home for the service.

Audience

The target audience is essentially people who love pets. This includes people who does not have time to give their pets the due attention and people who has enough time to take care of others pets and even make an additional income out of it.

Content

Home page

My Pets

Having a pet means having lots of love and loyal company at home, but we know that this can demand a lot of you and your family. Unlike most humans, your pet does not need material goods or academic studies to be happy, however, what he needs is something very precious that you have, your time and your attention. To ensure your pet is happy and healthy, in addition to feeding and vaccinations, you will need to spend some time with him to play and develop bonds. We know that it's not always possible to give them all the attention they deserve, that's why My Pets was created especially for people who love their pets and don't want them to be alone or miss out on the appropriate amount of care, love, and attention because of a busy routine. Here, these people will be able to find a fun, comfortable, and safe place where their pets will be taken care of by people who also love animals, while they are away taking care of their other responsibilities or even traveling. Our goal is to ensure the safety and well-being of your pet. Only here, at My Pets will you find people willing to take care of their pets the same way you would if you were with them.

Images for the Home page

Introducing: My Pets

Make Pet-Sitting Easy

Here, our customers will be able to search for other people willing to take care of their pets in various ways in order to find the perfect place for their pets. The main and easier option is a map, in which they will visualize the location of the people willing to provide this service whom we call "pet sitters", or if they prefer, they can filter the results according to the profile, including the type of place, allowed breeds and other characteristics. When you find the ideal pet sitter for your furry little one, you will be able to view the agenda and schedule an appointment according to their availability. Once scheduled, you'll have access to the pet sitter's profile and be able to exchange messages with them to match your pet's schedule and special needs. After defining the details of your pet's stay, payment can be made through the platform, according to the payment methods accepted by the pet sitter.

Find a Pet-Sitter

Our Partners Will Take Care of Your Loved Ones

All our pet sitters are registered in our database and go through a careful profile and background verification to ensure that they are able to provide a quality and reliable service so that you can have peace of mind while you are away from your loved little one. We make previous inspections in their places to make sure it is safe and has enough space to accommodate animals. You will also be able to see on the website if the pet sitter has any additional skills as a trainer or veterinarian. My Pets was created exclusively to provide connections between people who need someone to take care of their pets and for people who are available to take care of them in their places, if the pet sitter has some additional training these services can be negotiated outside the platform.

Important Information

To use My Pets services, make sure that your pet is adequately vaccinated and does not have any health restrictions to be in contact with other animals and other people. It is also important to ensure that he feels comfortable in the environment where he will stay, we recommend that you schedule a visit in advance so that your pet gets to know the new place and gets used to it. If the place already has other animals, it is important to ensure that all animals will maintain a peaceful and harmonious behavior.
