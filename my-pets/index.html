Find Your Pet Sitter

A place where people who love their pets can find other people who can take care of them with love.

Safe

Our Pet Sitters and their location are previously verified by us to make sure your pet will be in a safe place and taken care by trusted partners.

Easy

You can manage your reservations and appointments through our website, as well as keeping in touch with your Pet Sitter to receive frequent updates about your pet.

Quick

Use the map or filter to find the best place for your pet to stay. It will only take a few minutes for you to arrange and pay for your reservation.

Here, our customers will be able to search for other people willing to take care of their pets in various ways in order to find the perfect place for their pets. The main and easier option is a map, in which they will visualize the location of the people willing to provide this service whom we call "pet sitters", or if they prefer, they can filter the results according to the profile, including the type of place, allowed breeds and other characteristics. When you find the ideal pet sitter for your furry little one, you will be able to view the agenda and schedule an appointment according to their availability. Once scheduled, you'll have access to the pet sitter's profile and be able to exchange messages with them to match your pet's schedule and special needs. After defining the details of your pet's stay, payment can be made through the platform, according to the payment methods accepted by the pet sitter.
