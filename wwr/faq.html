Questions

How do I make reservations for our white water rafting?

Making reservations with us is easy! Decide which one of our exciting Outdoor Adventures you want to try at FastWater Sea Trips and what day you would like to come, then tell some friends. Be sure to check out our Events Calendar! Then call us or book online.

What is included in my white water raft trip?

With every raft trip at FastWater Sea Trips, guests will get a paddle, life jacket, bail bucket and water resistant bucket to use for the day. If you are on a Family Style Raft Trip from Memorial through labor day or on weekends in May, September or October, you will get a free riverside BBQ! If you are on the Upper or Lower Gorge Dam Release Whitewater Rafting Adventure, you can bring your own lunch or purchase lunch at the Broken Paddle Bar & Grill before your trip!

Are the river guides in our raft?

Our professional guides will be in kayaks alongside your raft. If you have special needs or feel you would like a river guide in the raft with you, we would be happy to do so for a $70.00 fee, based upon availability. Please call us at least two weeks prior to your trip date to check availability and have this service added to your reservation. Keep in mind that in-raft guides are not available on Whitewater Dam Release Saturdays in July & August.

Do we need to fill out a liability waiver to go white water rafting?

Yes. Every person that is rafting at FastWater Sea Trips is required to fill out one of our liability waivers when you arrive. All rafting guests that are under the age of 18 will also need a signature from a parent or guardian. Liability waivers that coordinate with your trip time will be given to each guest at check-in. Groups of 30 or more guests can download our liability waivers, fill them out, and bring them the day of your trip.
